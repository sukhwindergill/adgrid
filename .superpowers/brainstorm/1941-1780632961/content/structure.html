A — One page, audience toggle

Single URL. Toggle in hero switches all content below — how it works, benefits, FAQ — to the selected audience. Simpler to build and maintain. Nav links jump to the toggled view. Good when you can't drive traffic to specific URLs yet.

B — Home + two audience pages

Homepage has a split hero that routes each audience to their own page. Each page has full tailored copy, FAQ, and CTA. Better for SEO, better for conversion. Operators and advertisers never see irrelevant content. More to build but right long-term.
